About

To use this website, add users to the database using the Add Person link.

After adding everyone who will attend the dinner, create a new dinner on the Add Dinner page.

Assign the attendees to this dinner by selecting the desired dinner on the Add Person to Dinner dropdown.

After assigning at least four people to this dinner, click on the dinner in the Generate seating chart dropdown.

Manually place any diners who are supposed to sit in a specific place, then click on the submit button.

The dinner will be processed. After a few minutes, click on the Add Dinner page to view the seating arrangement.

If you want to make changes, delete the dinner and re-make it.
